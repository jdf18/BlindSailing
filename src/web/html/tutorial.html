How to Play Battleship

1. Objective

The goal of Battleship is to sink all of your opponent’s ships before they sink yours. Each player places ships strategically and takes turns guessing the opponent’s ship locations.

2. Game Setup

  • Ship Placement: Each player places five ships on their 10x10 grid:
    • Carrier - 5 cells
    • Battleship - 4 cells
    • Cruiser - 3 cells
    • Submarine - 3 cells
    • Destroyer - 2 cells
  • Rules:
    • Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically (not diagonally).
    • Ships cannot overlap each other.

3. Taking Turns

  1. Guessing: On your turn, choose a cell on the opponent’s grid to attack. For example, say "B4" to attack that cell.
  2. Hit or Miss: Your opponent will tell you if it’s a "hit" (a ship occupies that cell) or a "miss" (the cell is empty). Mark hits and misses on your grid for reference.
  3. Sinking Ships: When all cells of a ship are hit, that ship is "sunk." The opponent must declare the ship sunk.

4. Winning the Game

The first player to sink all five of their opponent’s ships wins the game!

5. Tips & Strategies

  • Spread out your ships to make them harder to find.
  • Track your hits and misses to avoid repeating guesses.
  • Focus on hits to sink ships faster by checking surrounding cells.
